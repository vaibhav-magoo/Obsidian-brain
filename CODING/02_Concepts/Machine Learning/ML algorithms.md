
## SUPERVISED LEARNING:
 you know the input and you know the output , the model's job is to figure out a relation between the two.
works on labelled data

### Classification:
	sort data into distinct categories called classes
**Common Examples:** *:
- examining an email and predicting if it is `Spam` or `Inbox`.
- Looking at an X-ray and predicting if a tumor is `Benign` or `Malignant`.
- Analyzing a transaction to classify it as `Fraudulent` or `Legitimate`.
### Regression:
	the algorithm's job is to predict a continuous numerical value
**Common Examples:**
- Predicting the `price of a house` based on its square footage and zip code.
- Forecasting the `exact temperature` for tomorrow.
- Estimating the `number of sales` a store will make next month.
